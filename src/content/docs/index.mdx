## Curriculum

We take a unique approach: starting with the **Visual** (what you can see), diving into the **Network** (how it travels), solidifying the **Logic** (how it works), and finally building the **Infrastructure** (where it lives).

## Prerequisites

There are no broad prerequisites for this entire course other than **Git** and a **Web Browser**.

- **Git**: [Install Git](https://git-scm.com/) to manage your code history.
- **Web Browser**: We strongly recommend [Google Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/) for its superior Developer Tools.

Each module may have specific requirements (like Node.js or a text editor), which will be listed in its introduction.
